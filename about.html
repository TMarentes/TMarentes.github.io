About T2 Book

Discover A World Of Tea Done Differently.

Purpose

T2 Book is a website that educates readers on the range of available flavours of T2 Tea. The official T2 Teas website makes it difficult to research and learn about teas; it focuses more on the purchasing of teas and giftware.

Our Goal

T2 Book provides tea-drinkers with a more accessible way to understand and read about tea flavours and profiles. As a T2 fan, it can be frustrating when browsing the official website to find detailed information, so this website aims to resolve that.
